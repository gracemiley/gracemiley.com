about
designer — salt lake city, ut

as a designer, i strive for a high level of sophistication and playfulness. every single part of the design process is carefully considered. i pride myself on conceptual, graphic, and technical skills — specifically through drawings, fabrication, and prototyping.

i have ample experience in branding, soft goods, and design research. i am a two-time winner of the design studio award in mdd; and been recognized by graphic and fashion designers in the industry.

i co-founded gria design studio with mia bookstaber — a multi-disciplinary creative studio working across branding, product design, and creative direction.
