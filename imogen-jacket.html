imogen jacket

a wearable design project rooted in memory — the brief was to design a garment inspired by a personal memory and take it all the way to manufacturing-ready documentation. the imogen jacket is named for and shaped by a specific emotional memory, translated into pattern, material, and construction. grace was the sole designer, responsible for concept, garment design, pattern making, and full tech pack production.

category soft goods · wearables · fashion design · fabrication
role sole designer
